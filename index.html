About

My name is Brian Siao Tick Chong (the last three words of my name are indeed my entire last name). I'm a tech enthusiast and like to keep myself busy in the heart of Boston. When it's not raining and the streets aren't covered in snow, you may find me speeding down Comm. Ave with my trusty, blue, non-motorized scooter (efficiency and timeliness is key).

I am a Computer Science major with a love for both music and visual art. My hobbies currently consist of playing video games, learning the guitar and ocarina, and dancing to K-pop. Check out the Profile section for more!

Music
